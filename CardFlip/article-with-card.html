International Stardom and NHL Buzz

Riihimäki's performances in Liiga did not go unnoticed by the national team selectors. He was called up to represent Finland at the World Junior Championship, where he led his team to a silver medal, finishing as one of the tournament's top scorers. His clutch performances, including a stunning hat-trick against Canada in the semifinals, solidified his status as a future star.

With the 2025 NHL Draft approaching, Riihimäki's name is at the top of nearly every scout's list. Projected as a potential top-five pick, he has drawn interest from several NHL teams, with franchises like the Chicago Blackhawks, Montreal Canadiens, and Detroit Red Wings reportedly considering him as a franchise cornerstone.

As Riihimäki continues to develop, the sky is the limit for this Finnish prodigy. Whether he stays in Liiga for another season or makes the jump to the NHL right away, one thing is clear—he is a name that hockey fans around the world should remember.
